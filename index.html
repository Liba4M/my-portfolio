My Projects

Global CO₂ Emissions Analysis

Tools Used: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

Passionate about sustainability, I explored the 'Our World in Data' CO₂ Emissions dataset to uncover global trends and insights. This project focused on visualising long-term CO₂ emission patterns across major countries, identifying top emitters by both total and per capita emissions, analysing the relationship between GDP and emissions, and breaking down emissions by fuel type—coal, oil, and gas. Through insightful visualisations and data wrangling, this project revealed global disparities and reinforced my passion for sustainability through data-driven impact.
